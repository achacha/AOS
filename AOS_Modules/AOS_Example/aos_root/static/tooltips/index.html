Top 5: Most Dangerous Creatures


1. There are many dangerous creature in the wild kingdom, but very few match up to tenacity, veracity and overall propensity to terror as the wild marmoset. Three of the most dangerous ones are Regular "Leaded" Marmoset , Pygmy "Unleaded" Marmoset , and White Headed "Diesel" Marmoset .
2. Another highly dangerous and destructive force commonly found decimating the wildlife around Mexico, the ferocious beast called a Ferocious Desert Chihuahua . Vicious looking indeed, few have lived to tell their tale of encounter with this monstrosity.
3. One cannot easily describe the sheer destructive prowess of the infamous Two Toed Death Sloth . A globally feared predator, easily annihilating prey, swooping down from above and just as quickly retracting to prepare for the next attack.
4. The Koala is a notoriously known as "The Plunder From Downunder". Vicious, remorseless and overall unpleasant. Many stories were told of the sheer devastation caused by one relatively hungry koala that decided that savaging one Eucalyptus tree was not enough. Generations still feel the effects of that incident.
5. No wild waters are safe when inhabited by the fecund beast known as a Guppy . Wily and devious, the pretend to be harmless until prey comes near, then their voracity easily surpasses the simple piranha. It is advised to stay out of any still or moving waters as the bodily harm that can be caused by these monsters is devastating.
